ORISA

Role: Tank

Orisa is an offensive tank equipped with a Fusion Driver and Energy Javelin to bombard enemies from long range. Her Javelin Spin and Fortify abilities increase her close-range survivability.
"Only in unity will we find strength."

Abilities


Augmented Fusion Driver

Automatic heat-based weapon.

Energy Javelin

Orisa throws a javelin forward, damaging and knocking back enemies hit, hitting a wall will increase damage taken.

Fortify

Orisa gains temporary health, reducing all damage taken and becoming unstoppable. While active, heat generated by her weapon is reduced.

Javelin Spin

Orisa spins an energy javelin in front of her, increasing her speed, knocking back enemies and destroying any incoming projectiles.

Ultimate


Terra Surge

Orisa raises her javelin into the air, spinning it above her head, which pulls in, slows and damages enemies near her, before stabbing it into the ground, dealing massive damage to enemies.
