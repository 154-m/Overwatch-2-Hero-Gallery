BRIGITTE

Role: Support

Brigitte specializes in armor. She can throw Repair Packs to heal teammates and automatically heals nearby allies when she damages foes. Her Rocket Flail can strike multiple close-range targets when swung normally, or a single medium-range target via the Whip Shot. When entering the fray, her Barrier Shield provides personal defense and can also be used offensively with Shield Bash.
"Breaking me down just builds me up."

Abilities


Inspire

Dealing damage to enemies heals nearby allies.

Rocket Flail

Brigitte's melee weapon has an extended range, enabling her to strike multiple enemies with a single swing.
